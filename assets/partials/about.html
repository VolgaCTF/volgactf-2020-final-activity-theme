VolgaCTF Activity regulations

Greetings everybody and welcome to VolgaCTF Activity!

To take part in the competition, you should sign up with an email address, your team's name (Latin characters only) and a logo. Warning! You will not be able to change your team's name once registered so please be mindful in order not to take an other team's name by accident. The number of team members is not limited.

The very top contestant/team physically present at VolgaCTF 2020 Final venue receives a prize.

Please specify a valid email address so as to get a confirmation email. It will be impossible to change the email address after you confirm your participation by clicking on a verification link. Note that the verification link expires in a few hours. A confirmation email with a new verification link may be requested on your team's profile page.

Warning! Disposable email addresses will not be approved of. A team taking a disposable email address may be disqualified.

The competition starts on September 15 at 10:00 SAMT / 06:00 UTC and lasts 36 hours. Flags match /VolgaCTF{[\x20-\x7F]+}/ unless stated otherwise. Check the News page at times – there might be some important pieces of information about the competition.

Competitors are forbidden to perform destructive actions against competition infrastructure (*.volgactf.ru and *.volgactf.org) and to prevent other teams from playing. A team violating the rules will be disqualified.

We reserve the right to amend these regulations at any time.
